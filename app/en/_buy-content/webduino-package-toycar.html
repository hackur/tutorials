How to Assemble Webduino Robot Car with Webduino Mark 1:

First connect ultrasonic sensor to Mark 1, VCC to 3.3V, Trig to pin 11, Echo to pin 10, and GND to GND. Then connect motor driver to Mark 1, VCC to VCC, B-B to pin 6, B-A to pin7, A-B to pin 8, and A-A to pin 9.
